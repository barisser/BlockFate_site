The Blockfate Protocol

Blockfate is a way for devices to monitor the Bitcoin Blockchain to establish access rights and payments information. Device permissions exist as abstracted digital tokens which may be moved from one Bitcoin address to another. By signing commands to devices with the private key controlling the access token, users cryptographically prove to devices that they are the legitimate owners.



Blockfate allows devices to:

  • Know with crpytographic certainty that they are receiving commands from their legitimate owner
  • Have their ownership transferred digitally anywhere in the world with virtually no transaction costs
  • Be rented out via Contracts to other addresses in exchange for bitcoin
  • Monitor the Blockchain for payments and act accordingly. Devices can be programmed to perform certain actions if any only if the appropriate payments are received.
  • Devices can store money as a safety deposit and return it upon completion of terms.
  • Trade access rights algorithmically with other devices. Machine-Machine transactions become possible.
